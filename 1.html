Muller Chapel:

A serene sanctuary with natural wood tones and floor-to-ceiling windows, offering a peaceful environment for relaxation and reflection. It also hosts various events and services for different faith communities.

East Tower:

The iconic 14-story towers that provide stunning views of the campus and the surrounding landscape. They feature single and double rooms, study lounges, and laundry facilities.

Egbert Hall:

A multi-purpose building that houses a dining hall, a cafe, and administrative offices. It is part of the Ithaca College Campus Center.
