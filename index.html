Ithaca College ~ Quick Facts

  • Pricing: ~$80,000 (Including Tuition, Housing, Health, and other expenses)
  • Location: Ithaca College 953 Danby Road Ithaca, NY 14850
  • Campus Culture: From stunning waterfalls, scenic hiking trails, and an award-winning farmers market to a “killer music scene,” Broadway-caliber theatre, eclectic restaurants, and a business startup incubator—it’s no wonder we were voted the #1 college town in the U.S.
  • Travel / Study Abroad: Locations in both Chicago & London, with study abroad / trave programs globally. "If you spin a glob and point somewhere, we can send you there to travel abroad. Evan at sea, as we offer a study at sea program!"
  • Acceptance Rate: ~ 69.94%
